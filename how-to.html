This quick guide will help you locate your chat.db file and run your first export.

Step 1: Find your iMessage Database

  • On your Mac, open Finder
  • Press Cmd + Shift + G
  • Paste: ~/Library/Messages
  • Look for these files:

    Important: Copy these files to your Desktop or another easy-to-find location before uploading.

    • chat.db
    • chat.db-wal
    • chat.db-shm
